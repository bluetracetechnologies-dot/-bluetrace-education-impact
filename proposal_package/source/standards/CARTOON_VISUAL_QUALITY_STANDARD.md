# Bluetrace Premium Cartoon Visual Standard

Purpose:
Create premium, realistic, boardroom-safe cartoon visuals for all Bluetrace app/product/service proposals.

## 1) Quality Bar (Non-Negotiable)
- Style: realistic illustration with cinematic lighting, not flat childish cartoons.
- Human anatomy: natural proportions, clean hands/faces, no distorted expressions.
- Environment detail: believable Indian school/business context (classroom, bus gate, principal office, parent home).
- Material realism: uniforms, ID cards, buses, phones, dashboards, desks should look authentic.
- Readability: every panel should be understandable in 3 seconds.

## 2) Color System (Brand-Safe)
Use this palette family across all storyboard panels:
- Primary Navy: #14324A
- Primary Teal: #1F8A70
- Accent Gold: #C58F2B
- Light Surface: #EEF4F7
- Soft Surface: #F7FAFC
- Text Dark: #24333F
- Text Muted: #5C6B73
- Border: #D5DEE5

Rules:
- Keep backgrounds soft and neutral; avoid neon gradients.
- Use gold only as highlight, not dominant fill.
- Use red only for critical alert states.
- Maintain WCAG-friendly contrast for text overlays.

## 3) Composition Rules
- Format: 16:9 for deck slides; 4:5 crops optional for social snippets.
- Safe margins: keep 8-10 percent edge padding for titles/captions.
- Character count per panel: 1-3 main subjects max.
- Focus hierarchy:
  1. Human emotion/problem
  2. Bluetrace intervention moment
  3. Outcome proof on app/dashboard

## 4) Typography and Captions
- Caption length: 6-12 words only.
- Tone: clear, executive-friendly, outcome-led.
- Avoid technical jargon in captions; keep technical detail in speaker notes.

## 5) Premium Realism Checklist
Before approval, each panel must pass:
- No extra fingers, broken limbs, or warped devices.
- App UI shown on phone/tablet is legible and coherent.
- Lighting direction and shadows are consistent.
- Uniform/logo usage is clean and non-distorted.
- Emotion is realistic (anxiety, relief, confidence, trust).

## 6) School Safety and Ethics Rules
- Event-based monitoring visuals, not surveillance-heavy framing.
- Show consent/responsible use cues where possible.
- No fear-based imagery; focus on trust and support.
- Child visuals must remain respectful and professional.

## 7) Deliverable Pack Per Storyboard
- 1 master slide per panel (PPTX editable).
- 1 export PNG per panel (1920x1080 minimum).
- 1 compact PDF strip (all panels with captions).
- 1 usage note with panel intent and KPI mapping.

## 8) Rejection Triggers
Reject and redo if:
- Style looks generic or low-detail.
- Colors break brand consistency.
- Panel message is unclear without verbal explanation.
- Characters or UI look artificial or inconsistent between panels.
- Visual quality drops at projector scale.

## 9) Duplicate Control and Versioning
- Keep only one master export per storyline in client-share folder.
- If two variants are visually equivalent, retain the higher readability one and archive the rest.
- File names must include version and revision (example: v1.0-R0).
- Before final handoff, run a quick duplicate check by thumbnail view and filename audit.
- Do not share draft duplicates with client-facing management decks.
