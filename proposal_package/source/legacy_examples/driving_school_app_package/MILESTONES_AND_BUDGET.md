- Budget: $15

- Budget: $17

5. M5 - QA, Stabilization, Documentation
- Budget: $5
- Duration: 1 day

Total: $100

## Note on Scope
The above plan is a fast MVP aligned to current posted budget. For full production rollout (payments, analytics dashboards, compliance workflows, multilingual support), a Phase-2 expansion plan should be created after MVP validation.
